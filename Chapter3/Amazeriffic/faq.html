Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

How do I change my password?
  • If you wish to change your password, please follow these steps:
  • Log in to your account
  • Select Hi, Your name at the top right of any xyz.com page
  • Select Profile
  • Select Edit next to the piece of information you wish to update
  • Enter your new information in the appropriate fields
  • Select Save to confirm changes
